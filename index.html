Jianing Mao

I am currently a final-year PhD student in Department of Electronic Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by Prof. Yuye Ling. Before that, I received the B.Eng. degree in Communication Engineering from Hunan University in 2020.

My research interests include biomedical optics and computational imaging. I am currently engaged in theoretical research on the interaction of light and tissue in Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT).
